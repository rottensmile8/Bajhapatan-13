Who We Are

A community built on service & solidarity

Shree Satya Yuwa Sewa Samiti is a youth-led social organisation from Bajhapatan, established in 2046 BS, dedicated to empowering marginalised communities, preserving culture, and driving sustainable change through grassroots initiatives.

We believe that real progress begins at home — in our streets, schools, and the hearts of our neighbours.

What We Do

Our key programs

🩸
Blood Donation

Organising regular blood donation camps to support hospitals and save lives across our district.

📚
Education Support

Providing free tutoring, school supplies, and scholarships to underprivileged children in Bajhapatan.

🌿
Environment

Tree plantation drives, waste management awareness, and clean-up campaigns to protect our environment.

🏥
Health Camps

Free medical check-ups, hygiene awareness, and mental health initiatives for local communities.

🤝
Disaster Relief

Rapid emergency response with food, clothing, and shelter support during floods and natural calamities.

🎭
Culture & Heritage

Preserving local festivals, traditions, and art forms through community celebrations and youth programs.
